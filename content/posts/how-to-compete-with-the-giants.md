the edge is not a religion — it’s a tool. in ai-driven apps across latam, smart placement beats blanket rules. care about latency, yes, but also about observability, costs, and failure modes.

- keep state simple
- measure tail latencies, not averages
- prefer boring tech when it wins reliability
